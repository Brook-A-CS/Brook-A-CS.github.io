Week of 02-Jan-2023:

Development Engineer:

The development engineer is responsible for coding the application. This means that they will have the most direct influence on the implementation. This is the most time consuming role as features will need to constantly be added, removed, and revised with the development engineer receiving criticism from almost every other role. I am suitable for this role because I am willing to put in the required hours in order to ensure that we meet deadlines as well as learning NodeJS which will be what we use to develop our website. A challenge I expect to encounter will be meeting deadlines which will require long hours on those days in order to meet them. Other challenges will mostly be technical challenges which I plan to overcome through research and careful planning.

QA Engineer

The QA engineers review the code to ensure it meets quality standards. Also ensures that code works in all use cases. They work closely with development engineers in order to help build up a strong codebase which will be reliable and scalable. I am suitable for this role due to my strong problem solving skills and attention to detail helping me debug software. On top of this, I have strong communication skills which helps when giving suggestions to teammates. Challenges I expect to encounter are frustration when working on a single bug for a long period of time. I plan on overcoming this by taking a short break in order to look at the issue with fresh eyes. Another challenge is, as the project progresses and becomes larger, knowledge of all the components will be essential to debugging parts of it. I plan to overcome this by staying on top of everything that is made, including those made by team members.

Project Manager:

The project manager oversees what everyone is doing and what features are being worked on by each team member. This role needs a strong vision of the project just like the product manager however with the added responsibility of understanding what team members can do and in what time period. This role will also need to keep the product manager realistic in their expectations if necessary such as what features can be added and the overall scope of the project. I am suitable for this role because I am comfortable with creating detailed planners while simultaneously understanding that flexibility is sometimes required. A challenge I expect to encounter will be finding times where all team members can be present at the same time. Since I am in a separate class period than my team members there might be issues finding time to meet up. I plan on overcoming this by communicating online which will give us a much more flexible amount of time to communicate.

Product Manager:

Oversees what features will be implemented and gives a general idea of when they should be done. This is the high level research and planning that will be necessary in order to give the team the broad idea of what the project will become.I am suitable for this role because I have a very strong vision of what this project should be and know the technologies we need to use in order to meet this vision. My knowledge in coding will also help set realistic roadmaps for the team. One challenge I might face is the inevitable necessity to choose when a feature needs to be removed due to not meeting expectations or no longer being needed. I plan on overcoming this by discussing this with my team members and communicating my reasoning for my decisions.

Release Engineer:

The release engineer determines everything from how the software is stored to build rules and guidelines, as well as final deployment. For this project, the most important and frequently used part of this role will be source control such as how the github is organized as well as being in charge of major merges. I am suitable for this role due to my experience and comfort in using git source control. One challenge of this role is the technical challenge of source control ensuring the correct code is used and nothing necessary is overwritten. This is overcome through experience and research over various git commands and their functions, and at the end, everything is stored and can be reverted. A major challenge of this role is the necessary communication with every other role because a chosen branch strategy must be agreed upon and understood by everyone.
